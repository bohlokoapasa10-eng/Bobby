Background

Bobby is a store that sells products that are essential for the well-being of dogs.The products offered include shampoos, combs , dog food,collars and medication for dogs. The store aims to improve the well-being of dogs, the founder of the business is Bohlokoa. The name of the business sterms from Bohlokoa's first dog , whose name is Bobby.
